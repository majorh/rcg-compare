Crown & Anchor Society

Earn 1 point per night sailed. Suite guests earn 2 points per night. Complimentary upgrades do not earn double points. Benefits are cumulative as you move up tiers.

How Points Work: 1 Cruise Point = 1 night in a standard cabin. 2 points/night in a suite (when booked at suite rate). Solo travelers paying double occupancy also earn 2 points/night. Kids under 18 may share family status until age 18, but cannot reach Pinnacle on family points alone.
